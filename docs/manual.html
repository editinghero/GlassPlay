Maximize your viewing experience by switching to fullscreen mode during video playback and editing.

Methods to Enter Fullscreen(Ambient mode):


  1. Click the fullscreen button in the top right corner of the home page

  1. Click the fullscreen button in the video controls (toolbar)

üé¨ Ambient Video Sync

Learn how the ambient background video automatically syncs with your main video playback controls for a seamless viewing experience.

How Ambient Video Sync Works:


  1. Automatic Synchronization: The ambient background video automatically syncs with the main video player
  2. Play/Pause Control: When you pause the main video, the ambient video also pauses simultaneously
  3. Resume Playback: When you resume the main video, the ambient video continues from the same position
  4. Seek Synchronization: When you skip or seek to different parts of the video, the ambient video follows

Ambient Video Features:


  1. Low Resolution Processing: Ambient video is processed at 144p for smooth performance
  2. Blur Effect: The background video is automatically blurred to create an immersive atmosphere
  3. FFmpeg Required: Ambient video sync only works when FFmpeg processing is enabled
  4. Real-time Sync: No delay between main video and ambient video controls

Controlling Ambient Video:


  1. Play/Pause: Use spacebar, click play button, or any video control method
  2. Seeking: Click on the progress bar or use arrow keys to jump to different timestamps
  3. Speed Control: Change playback speed - ambient video will match the same speed
  4. Volume Control: Ambient video has no audio, only the main video audio plays
üí° Important Notes:
‚Ä¢ Ambient video sync requires FFmpeg to be enabled during video upload
‚Ä¢ If FFmpeg is disabled, only the main video will play without ambient background
‚Ä¢ The sync is automatic - no manual configuration needed
‚Ä¢ Both videos maintain perfect synchronization throughout playback
üéØ Troubleshooting:
‚Ä¢ If ambient video doesn't sync: Ensure FFmpeg was enabled during video processing
‚Ä¢ For URL streams: Download the video first, then enable FFmpeg for ambient processing
‚Ä¢ Check Task Manager for ffmpeg.exe process during video upload
